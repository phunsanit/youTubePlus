Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not send any data to external servers owned by us.

How the Extension Works

YouTubePlus performs the following actions:

  • Tab Access: The extension reads the URLs of your open browser tabs to identify YouTube video pages. This information is only processed locally in your browser.
  • YouTube API: When you choose to save videos, the extension uses the official YouTube Data API with your Google account authorization to add videos to your personal playlist. This communication is directly between your browser and Google's servers.
  • Authentication: The extension uses Google OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with YouTube. Your login credentials are handled entirely by Google; we never see or store your password.

Data Storage

The extension does not store any data on external servers. All operations are performed locally in your browser session.

Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • YouTube Data API (Google): Used to manage your playlists. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions Explained

  • tabs: Required to detect open YouTube video tabs in your browser.
  • identity: Required for Google OAuth authentication to access YouTube API on your behalf.
  • Host permissions (youtube.com, googleapis.com): Required to interact with YouTube pages and the YouTube API.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You can revoke the extension's access to your YouTube account at any time through your Google Account Settings.
