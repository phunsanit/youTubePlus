## Extension Name
YouTubePlus - Save Videos to Playlist

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Find open YouTube video tabs, save them to your personal playlist with one click, and auto-close tabs. Simple and efficient!

## Detailed Description
YouTubePlus is a simple browser extension that helps you manage your YouTube watching experience.

**Features:**
• Automatically detects all open YouTube video tabs
• Saves videos to a personal "My Watch Later" playlist
• Automatically closes tabs after saving
• Works with your Google account via secure OAuth

**How to use:**
1. Open multiple YouTube videos in different tabs
2. Click the YouTubePlus icon in your toolbar
3. Click "Save All to Watch Later"
4. Videos are saved and tabs are closed automatically!

**Privacy:**
This extension only accesses YouTube tabs and uses the official YouTube API to save videos to your playlist. No data is collected or shared.
